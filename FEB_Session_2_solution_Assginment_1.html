Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods.Here is a recipie for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the cream and eggs in a bowl.
  3. Once the butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from heat(it will continue to cook until served)
